# Wh questions

To ask about something more specific ou get more information we can use the "wh" questions

    "wh" +  "do" + "person" + "verb" ?

| wh    | goal  | example                      |
|-------|-------|------------------------------|
| What  | Thing | What team do you support?    |
| When  | Time  | When do you play volleyball? |
| Where | Place | Where do they volleyball?    |
