Question Structure

  1. WH word + auxiliary verb + subject + main verb

    • What do you want?

    • Where does she live?

    • When will they arrive?

  2. WH word + be verb + subject

    • Who is that?

    • Where are you?

    • What is this?

Common Patterns

Present Simple

  • What do you do?

  • Where does she work?

  • When do they play?

Past Simple

  • What did you say?

  • Where did he go?

  • When did it happen?

Present Continuous

  • What are you doing?

  • Where is she going?

  • Why are they running?
